How it Works

Job Seekers

Signing up!

Before you sign up please read our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.On signup, make sure you provide accurate and honest information about yourself as much as possible. Provide your personal details, qualifications, skills, work experience, languages, references and a photo of your face. You are encouraged to attach a CV document in Microsoft word or Pdf format.

Job Seeker Sign Up

How to Search and apply for jobs!

You can search for jobs without signing in. However to apply for jobs you need to have an existing account, and signed in. You can search for jobs by providing just the job title, e.g. Electrical Engineer or Management Accountant. You can also search for jobs by providing both the job title, and job location. To search for jobs click on Jobs in the main menu. When applying online, please provide a cover note describing how you fit the job requirements. Some recruiters might ask jobseekers to send applications directly to their e-mails instead of applying online.

How to manage your profile!

To manage your profile, click on My Profile in the main menu. You can add, edit and delete your personal details, qualifications, skills, work experience, languages, references and personal face photo.


Recruiters

Signing up!

Before you sign up please read our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.On signup please provide your company details as much as possible. Make sure you provide your company logo as it will make your job adverts look professional and authentic.

Recruiter Sign Up

How to update recruiter company details!

To update your company details, click on Recruiters, then on Recruiter Details and finally on Edit Details button. You will be directed to the Edit Recruiter Details. Update the details and click on the Update button.

How to create a job ad!

To create a job ad , click on Recruiters then on Post Job Ad. Fill up all the required job ad details, and proceed to preview the job ad. To create the job ad without proceeding to Checkout and payment, click on the Save button. To create job ad and proceed to pay for just that job ad, click on Save & Add to new order button. You will be directed to the Checkout page where you should click on Pay now to pay for your order. To create a job ad and add to a group of job ads in an order and pay for those job ads, click on Save & Add to existing order, select the order to add to and click on Add to this order button. You will be directed to the Checkout page where you should click on Pay now to pay for your order. The easiest and fastest way to create a job ad is to copy an existing job ad.To do this, click on Recruiters then on Manage your job Ads. You will be directed to the Manage your Job Ads page where you can search for your job ads. Click on Copy and Create New button to copy the job ad you want.You will be directed to the Create Job Ad page where you will have to fill up just a few details like the JobREF ,Closing Date and How to apply instructions. On our system you can create a job ad today and decide to pay another day. All job Ads are stored on our system database.

How to edit a Job Ad!

To edit a job add navigate to the Manage your Job Ads page by clicking on Recruiters, then on Manage your Job Ads. You can search for your job ads using parameters like JobREF and Job Title. Select the job ad you want to edit, and you will be directed to the job ad edit page. Update the details and click on preview job ad. On the preview page, click on Update to just update without proceeding to payment. To update job ad and proceed to pay for just that job ad, click on Update & Add to new order button. To update a job ad and add to a group of job ads in an order and pay for those job ads, click on Update & Add to existing order, select the order to add to and click on Add to this order button

How to Manage your Job Ads!

To manage your job Ads, click on Recruiters , then on Manage your Job Ads. You can search for all your job ads or by JobREF, JobTitle, Date, Date Range. You can also search for job ads that are still open or closed, paid for or not yet paid for. You can edit or delete your existing job ads. You can also mark your job ads as closed so that candidates will not be able to send online applications.

How to Manage your Orders!

To manage your Orders click on Recruiters then on Manage your Orders. You can check which orders have been paid for or not been for paid. You can also check which job ads are included in each order. On this page, you can choose an order to pay for and proceed straight to CheckOut.

Payments!

Currently, job ads orders payment can only be made via Credit Cards/Cheque Cards (Mastercard,VISA). All payments are made through Payfast, one of South Africa’s leading online payment gateways. On the CheckOut page, when you click on the Pay Now button, you will be re-directed to the Payfast website where you are required to enter your credit card details. After successful payment you will be re- directed back to the Jobmix website success page.If payment was cancelled,you will be re-directed to the CheckOut page. The payment system is fully automated without human intervertion. However, if you face any problems, please send e-mail enquiries to jobmixSA@gmail.com or phone +27(0) 62 345 9793. Jobmix.co.za , and the order number will appear on your bank statements for every payment.

How to Manage Job Applications!

If you enabled apply online on some job ads, you will be able to receive job applications online. To view these applications navigate to the Manage Job Applications page by clicking on Recruiters then on Manage Job Applications. You are able to search for all applications you have received in your account. To search for applications for a particular job ad, use the job REF you used when creating that job ad. If you are not sure about the job REF, just use the job title to search. All applications for job ads with the job title you used will be displayed. Select the job applications of interest. You are able to view the individual applications and also the profiles of the candidates who applied. Each job application will have the job ad REF, job title, the name of the applicant, a link to the applicant profile ,as well as date of application. You are also able to delete individual applications.

How to Search for Candidates and Manage your candidates!

Searching for suitable candidates on our system is absolutely free. However ,you may fail to find candidates of interest since Jobmix.co.za is a new kid on the block. To search for suitable candidates, click on Recruiters then on Search for Candidates.You are able to search for all candidates on our system.You can search for candidates of interest by using the following filters: Qualification, Skill, Profession, and Preferred Job Titles. After searching for candidates of interest you are able to store their details in your own database by clicking on the add CV button. To view this database later click on Recruiters then on Manage your candidates.You are able to view the profiles of these candidates as well as deleting them from your database.
